Short Description Of Our Destionation

For those of you who travel to Paris with children and family, you can choose Disneyland Paris attractions. Previously called Euro-Disney, this favorite amusement park has been open since April 12, 1992. Today, Disneyland Paris provides attractions and rides with advanced technology so that many tourists visit. In this tourist area, visitors can find various kinds of favorite Disney characters such as Mickey Mouse, Toys Story, Lion King, Alddin, and many more. You can enjoy various interesting tourist attractions such as Adventure Isle, Big Thunder Mountain, Meet Mickey Mouse, and many more. Then, at this favorite tourist spot, there are many festivals that must be attended such as The Lion King and Jungle Festival, Disney Halloween Parties, Frozen Celebration, and many more. In addition, there are many food restaurants and cafes with Disney themes, which will certainly add an unforgettable experience for children.
